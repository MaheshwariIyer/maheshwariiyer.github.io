Vertias

A fake news detector, a website, and app to help you avoid falling for fake news and click bait.

Scroll down for project info.

Project

Veritas Website and Mobile App

Main roles

Application functionality, coding of app and website, Wireframing, connecting print functionality.

The Brief

In the 20th century, technology has made it easier to spread ideas faster,making propaganda reach more in numbers. The incarnation of fake news would not be possible without the digital media tools available today. Due to the constant repetitions of news to users, fact-checking has become a thing of the past. News and Social Media has played major roles in Politics, Social and Financial sectors, influencing the majority’s decision with no backup or factual correctness. Fake news has easily penetrated the daily apps and websites we visit, Facebook, Snapchat, Instagram through various forms, one the famous of them is clickbait.

Launch project

SOLUTION

This has given rise to an opportunity to enable a small-scale fact check mechanism. It is an interactive art piece involving a mobile-based application, with a listing mix of news – fake and true. When the user clicks on the news they desire to fact check, a static screen will appear, and as a result, the page is automatically printed out. The hope is that users with this small level interaction will attain the skills with them to spot fake news when they see it elsewhere. It will also help users increase their f amiliarity with fake news distribution and be immune to future manipulation. Allowing them to question how clickbait has been deceiving them over the years.
