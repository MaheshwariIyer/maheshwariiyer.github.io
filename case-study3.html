We developed a website-Chinatown Walk which focused on a walk through the history of Melbourne 's Chinatown.

Project

Chinatown Melb Walk

Main roles

Project Manager, Icons and Images, Wireframing, Promo Video, Maps.

The Brief

Chinatown is rich in history and culture, using the Mid Autumn Festival as an event, we decided to launch the Chinatown walk, a website. The user is required to use the map in the website to walk through Chinatown, using 5 important locations we have created 5 infographics describing the history of Chinatown. We wanted to make this an interactive and engaging website, to educate the locals as well as tourists.
