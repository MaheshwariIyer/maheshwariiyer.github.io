Namaste

I'am an Indian creative designer currently studying digital media design at RMIT, Melbourne. I strive to build brands, experiences that people believe in. My voyage began with founding a custom merchandises company. Continued to create posters, graphics, brochures, logo design mainly for social media accounts of corporate companies. Throughout the past 2 years, my focus has been on designing and implementing user experiences that are accessible and sustainable. My main areas of expertise are research, prototyping, and user testing. This also gave me the opportunity to understand web design and indulge in coding. I'm also pursuing motion design as an area of interest, from video shoots to explainers videos and animations. I strive to be a thinker and solver of problems. I always loved the unexpected, loved new ideas.
